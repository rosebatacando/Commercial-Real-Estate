The Basics

What Is A Syndication?

A group of investors pool capital to acquire a property too large — or too expensive — for any one of them to buy alone.

One sponsor finds, underwrites, and runs the deal. We co-invest alongside every investor, so our interests move together.

Know The Players

GP vs. LP

Sponsor

General Partner

  • Sources & underwrites the deal
  • Raises the capital
  • Manages the asset day-to-day
  • Co-invests 5–10% of equity
Investor

Limited Partner

  • Provides most of the capital
  • Fully passive, no management
  • Liability capped at investment
  • Usually 70–90% of equity

Where Profits Go

The Distribution Waterfall

LPs typically earn a preferred return (commonly 8%) before the GP earns anything. After that, profit splits — often 70/30 in the LP's favor.

LP invests$100,000
8% pref return$8,000/yr, paid first
Profit above prefsplit 70/30
LP receivespref + 70% of upside
